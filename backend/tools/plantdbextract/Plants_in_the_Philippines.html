Zygophyllaceae

Tribulus L. (Not native)

Last edited by Pieter B. Pelser, 22 July 2023
  1. Tribulus cistoides L., Sp. Pl., 1st Ed. (1753) 387; Merr., Fl. Manila (1912) 268; Merrill, EPFP 2 (1923) 326; Steenis, Fl. Males. ser. 1, 4 (1949) 64. Distribution: Africa, Australia, China, Indian Ocean, Java, Lesser Sunda Isls, Madagascar, Moluccas, New Caledonia, New Guinea, Pacific Ocean, Taiwan, Vietnam. BORACAY, LUZON: La Union (photos), PALAWAN, PANAY. Notes: Naturalized in waste places and along the seashore and on beaches. Naturalized.
